Baked Salmon

Description

This baked salmon recipe is a simple and healthy way to enjoy this delicious fish. It features a blend of herbs and spices that enhance the natural flavors of the salmon.

Baking uses less oil, hence making it healthier while being complementary with freshly cooked rice!

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon of dried basil or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of salt or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper or to taste
  • 2 (6-ounce) salmon fillets

Steps

  1. Whisk olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, parsley, basil, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl.
  2. Arrange salmon fillets in a small glass or ceramic baking dish; pour marinade over salmon. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for about 1 hour, turning occasionally.
  3. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  4. Transfer salmon fillets onto a large piece of aluminum foil. Spoon marinade on top and fold up the foil to seal. Place sealed foil packs on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake in preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 20 to 25 minutes.
  6. Optional: Serve with freshly cooked rice for a hearty meal!
