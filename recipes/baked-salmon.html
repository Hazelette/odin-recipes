Baked Salmon

Description

This baked salmon recipe is a simple and healthy way to enjoy this delicious fish. It features a blend of herbs and spices that enhance the natural flavors of the salmon.

Baking uses less oil, hence making it healthier while being complementary with freshly cooked rice!

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon of dried basil or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of salt or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper or to taste
  • 2 (6-ounce) salmon fillets
